# SnapifY - The Modern Event Sharing Platform

## Executive Summary

**SnapifY** is a revolutionary event sharing platform that transforms how people capture, share, and experience memories at events. Unlike traditional photo sharing apps that require downloads and complex setups, SnapifY enables frictionless participation through QR codes and instant real-time sharing.

**Problem**: Traditional event photography is cumbersome, expensive, and disconnected. Guests miss moments while fumbling with apps, and event organizers struggle with inconsistent photo quality and delayed delivery.

**Solution**: SnapifY provides instant, professional-grade event photography with zero friction - guests simply scan and snap, photos appear instantly on live screens.

- **Event Organizers**: Expensive professional photographers, inconsistent results, delayed delivery
